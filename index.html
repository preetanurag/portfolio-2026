Personal Projects

Building something close to my heart

Wealth Desk AI-enabled portfolio analysis and management

Wealth Desk is the kind of project that says a lot about me: part engineering playground, part investing dashboard, part AI experiment, and part attempt to make personal finance less scattered.

  • Tracks stocks, mutual funds, FD/RD, watchlists, results, and consolidated portfolio views.
  • Syncs broker workflows for Zerodha with live price updates and market-data services.
  • Uses OpenAI-powered portfolio intelligence for Q&A, contextual insights, and follow-up analysis.
  • Lets users define strategy-driven watchlists and discover stocks through AI-assisted search.
